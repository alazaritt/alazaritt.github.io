## Overview

The schematic illustrates how the system effectively converts audio input from a microphone into a control signal for a separate board to operate lights, fulfilling both user needs and product requirements. The microphone captures sound, while the amplification and filtering circuitry ensure the signal is clean enough to avoid false signals, allowing for reliable and responsive operation.
